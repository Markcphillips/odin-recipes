Marinated Flank Steak

If you want a tender, juicy, flavoured steak it needs to be marinated for at least two hours.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • ¼ cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 ½ tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (1 1/2-pound) flank steak

Steps

  1. Whisk oil, soy sauce, vinegar, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, garlic, and pepper.
  2. Place steak in a glass baking dish and add the marinade.
  3. Place in a fridge from 2 to 12 hours.
  4. Preheat your outdoor grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Remove steak from the marinade and shake off the excess.
  6. Cook the stake for five minutes on each side.
  7. Remove from heat and rest for five minutes
  8. Slice, serve enjoy!
